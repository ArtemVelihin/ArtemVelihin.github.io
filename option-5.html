Risk Warning

Investment Risks: Investing in small public listed or private companies involves significant risks including illiquidity, lack of dividends, loss of investment and equity dilution. Invest only as part of a diversified portfolio.

No FSCS Protection: Investments on the Crowd for Angels platform are not covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme.
