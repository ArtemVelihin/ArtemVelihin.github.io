Risk Warning

Capital at Risk: Investing in small companies involves significant risks including illiquidity, loss of investment, lack of dividends, and equity dilution. Invest only as part of a diversified portfolio.

No FSCS Protection: Investments are not covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme. If the company fails, you may lose your entire investment.

Tax Relief: SEIS/EIS availability depends on individual circumstances and may be subject to change. Seek independent tax advice.
